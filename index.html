Want to know about your delivery and logistics charges?
Our delivery charge is designed according to the weight of the parcel. Charges start from Tk 60 per kg inside Dhaka, Tk 100 around Dhaka and Tk 130 outside Dhaka. 1% cash on delivery charge is applicable for every delivery near and outside Dhaka.
